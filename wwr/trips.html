Splash White Water Rafting Company

Splash starts high in the Rockies and comes roaring out of the mountains. When its water levels rise in spring and summer, it creates a huge variety of incredible white water — and the rafting action is non-stop. Any rafting guide will tell you that the Splash will keep you on your toes!
