Splash White Water Rafting Company

Splash starts high in the Rockies and comes roaring out of the mountains. When its water levels rise in spring and summer, it creates a huge variety of incredible white water — and the rafting action is non-stop. Any rafting guide will tell you that the Splash will keep you on your toes!

Our certified guides prioritize your safety with top-quality equipment and comprehensive training. Every adventure begins with a detailed safety briefing to ensure you're prepared for the excitement ahead.

Discover untouched wilderness and breathtaking landscapes as you navigate through crystal-clear waters. Our carefully selected routes showcase the most spectacular scenery nature has to offer.

Expert Guides

Learn from the best with our team of experienced, certified guides who know every rapid, rock, and story along our rivers. They'll make your adventure both thrilling and educational.

Family Adventures

Create lasting memories with adventures suitable for all skill levels. From gentle floats perfect for families to adrenaline-pumping rapids for thrill-seekers, we have something for everyone.
